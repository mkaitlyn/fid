Founded in 2001 by Mr.PocketKnife FailedMage, Jinx & Tonic is commited to providing quality potions at outragous prices. Being the one and only underground potion shop in the downtown area, its our responsibility and privilage to supply and deliver these products to our customers without a cent of legal suspicion. You can count on us to be speedy and stealthy on deliveries and usually not rat you out to the law if you get on our nerves. We encourage you to do the same. As quoted by Mr. FailedMage: "We may not be the best, but we sure charge like it. And if you dont like it? Perish."

Mr. PocketKnife FailedMage takes special care to support and source the majority of our products from local businesses, many of which are manufactured right here in Gulzvurwhal. You can even find elixers made lovingly in house by our questionable Alchemy staff. Delivery fees are waved on orders of 20 potions or more. Delivery fees are doubled if we find out that you are rich and can afford it, or if you are rude to our staff.

Jinx & Tonic nor Mr. PocketKnife FailedMage will be responsible for any adverse side effects from samples that you consume directly or secondhand. There is an extra charge to participate in Sample Night, but we wil only tell you about it after youve already taken a sip. Absolutely no drinking under an invisability spell, and please have a designated driver pick you up before closing.
